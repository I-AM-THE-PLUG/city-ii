Welcome to City Institute of Higher Learning

Where education meets innovation and diversity. Located in the heart of Moncton, C-IHL offers a variety of programmes tailored to foster global leaders equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary for success in today's dynamic world. Our commitment to excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement makes C-IHL the perfect place to start or enhance your educational journey. Join us and become part of a community that values academic integrity and inclusivity.

We are Atlantic Canada's Bilingual Private Institute of Higher Learning.

  • We are a research-based learning institution.
  • We are powered by a global team.
  • We are here to serve you.
  • Quality is us.
