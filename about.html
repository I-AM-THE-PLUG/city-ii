About City Institute of Higher Learning

At City Institute of Higher Learning (CIHL), we pride ourselves on fostering an environment of academic excellence and innovation. Established in 2013, CIHL has quickly become a beacon of learning in Moncton, embracing both local and those studying virtually from abroad. Our mission is to equip students with the critical thinking and professional skills needed to excel in a globalized world.

City Institute of Higher Learning (C-IHL) is an advanced research-focused education institute in New Brunswick, Canada, that serves the needs of the research community and businesses. C-IHL is part of the Institute of International Education Canada (IEC) Inc., founded in 2013. The Institute is a significant place for scholarly research and business consultancy covering contemporary global issues. C-IHL focuses primarily on advanced research and management learning. We aim to produce graduates who are equipped with exceptional skills to serve local and international development needs. Most of our research, courses and consultancy activities can be taken in English and French. We are one of the few private institutes that offer this flexibility.

Our Mission

To serve our diverse and dynamic community by providing high-quality educational programs that combine rigorous academic standards with real-world experience. We aim to nurture and develop the leaders of tomorrow, ensuring they are prepared for the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leader in global education, known for our commitment to student success, research excellence, and community engagement. We strive to create an inclusive, innovative, and inspiring learning environment that promotes lifelong learning.

Institute Timeline

Each phase of our history reflects our ongoing commitment to adapt and respond to the educational needs and aspirations of our community, continually striving to offer more relevant, impactful, and accessible education.

2013

Company Established

From Institute of Chinese & Asian Research (ICAR)

In the early years, our journey began as the Institute of Chinese & Asian Research (ICAR), a hub dedicated to enhancing understanding and collaboration through focused research on Chinese and Asian studies. This period marked our initial commitment to fostering international ties and understanding through education.

2015

Institute of International Education Canada (IEC)

As our scope expanded and our mission evolved to include a broader global perspective, we transitioned to the Institute of International Education Canada (IEC). This name change reflected our growing emphasis on offering a diverse range of international educational programs and our dedication to creating global opportunities for our students.

2024

City Institute of Higher Learning (C-IHL)

In 2024, we embraced a new identity as the City Institute of Higher Learning (C-IHL), signifying a comprehensive approach to higher education that merges research, practical knowledge, and innovative learning strategies. This name encapsulates our commitment to providing top-tier education that prepares students for success in a rapidly changing global landscape. At C-IHL, we continue to build on our foundation of research excellence and community engagement, while adapting to the ever-evolving educational needs of our students around the world.
